Autumn Leaf Carving

The autumn leaf pumpkin is a invaiting pumpkin into the autumn time of the year. Its a very hard pumpkin to do. The steps are:

  1. Find or pring out on paper shapes on leafs u wan't to incarv onto ur pumpkin
  2. Draw on to the pumpkin the outline of the leafs and the stems in the leaf
  3. Carv the skin of the pumpkin with the inprint of the leaf/leafs
  4. (Optional) Carv the top of the pumpkin and emty out the inside (For inside light or just to make the pumpkin lighter)
