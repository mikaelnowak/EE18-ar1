Skeleton Marquee Pumpkin

A hard but elegant and if it is dune corectly, it can last a long time. Its also wery timeconsuming and you will need to spend a lot of money to make a nice design. The picture is a example you can make for $20-$30. The tutorial is just a way to show the principe of making this design. The steps are:

  1. Cut and empty out from the side of the pumpkin
  2. Make so that the whole is in the final for the whole
  3. Inline the whole pumpkin flesh with a type of insolation fome or silicon spray so the flesh will not rotten.
  4. Use a carton inside the pumpkin with will be used als a wall wich will hold your decorations on
  5. (Optional) You dont need to insulate the flesh if u dont want, but the building prosess will be hard and the livespand with the carton will be a copple of days
