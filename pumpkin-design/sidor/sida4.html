Cat Drilled Pumpkin

This pumpkin can be used with other animal heads or shapes, but for deminstration we used cat heads. It's much simpler then you think. You make this but:

  1. Carving the top and empty out the inside
  2. Color the outside of the pumpkin with wich color u wan't
  3. Print out the template of the cape or thing u wan't on the pumpkin
  4. Mark and carv out the shape from the pumpkin
