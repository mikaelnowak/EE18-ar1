Pumpkin Candy Dispensor

The candy dispensor is a nice whay to give the kids who is going to trick or treat without leaving whatever you are doing just to give them ther candy. You can just leave it beside the door and forget about it. The steps to make this simple pumpkin design is:

  1. Find a pumpkin which is bigger then container you are going to have in the pumpkin
  2. Mark the side with you're going to intert the container in with a pensil or scharpe
  3. Cut the marked area and get the inside of the pumpkin empty
  4. Plase your container in the whole you cut out and fill it to the brim with candy
  5. Plase the pumpkin on it's final plase
