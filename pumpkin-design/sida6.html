Pumpkin Vampire Candles

A nice way to light op the house or inside. Thay are a pritty nice but it's very "Halloweene" wich meens that it's not like a design you can use all year long. The steps are:

  1. Bye the small pumpkins (Big one's don't work)
  2. Carv the theeth in a triangular shape with one point heding down
  3. Mark the eye's with a black dye like inc and have them a little more therder appart from the theeth's
  4. Take a small, red candle and place it on the top of the pumpkin
