Pumpkin Candy Jar

A pritty replasement of a jar or bowl u can have inside or outside. It's also safe for kids because thay will not shater if someone brakes it. The steps are:

  1. Cut the top of the pumpkin and empty out the inside
  2. Color both the outside and inside of the pumpkin with a color of your chois (Let it dry before going onto the next step)
  3. Fill the pumpkin with candy of your chois
  4. (Optional) You can wright or draw what u wan't on the pumpkin if you want to
