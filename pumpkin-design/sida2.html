Honeycomb Pumpkin

The Honeycomb design is a little bit hard. But it gives the pumpkin a whole new style and it's a really unick and fun way to spend the time with friends and family. To make the honeycomb pumpkin, you need to:

  1. Carv the top of the pumpkin and get out the inside without taking of the pumpkin flesh
  2. Mark the honeycomb spots with some sort of hexagon-shaped figur or template
  3. Carv some honeycomb's all the way throo the pumpkin (Dont cut if the honeycomb's will touth etch other)
  4. the remaining comb's you need to carv just the skin
  5. (Optional) You can take some yellow paper and cut them out with a hexagon shape and attach the form in the middle of all the non-hollow honeycomb's
